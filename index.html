FAQ

Q: What does ScreenD do?


A: ScreenD is an app that helps you with your online security. We remember your passwords so you don't have to and teach you about the dangers you will come across online.


Q: Why is it important to be safe online?


A: Companies take your data and know everything about you nowadays. Some sell your data and make money off of it. It is important to know which apps know what about you.


Q: Does ScreenD also have access to my personal data?


A: ScreenD does not gather any data on its own. If you wish to use the password saver function it will save your password data, this data is inaccessible by anyone and is only visible to you.
